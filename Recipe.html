Grandma's Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips
  • Optional:
    1. 1/2 cup of nuts
    2. 1/4 cup of raisins

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. In a seperate bowl, mix the wet ingredients.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, then add chocolate chips.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.

Tips and Variations

  • Use brown sugar for a richer flavor.
  • Add nuts for extra crunch.
  • Replace butter with coconut oil for a vegan option.
